# aTale: stories behind things

## Introduction

In a world where stories shape our identities and memories are our most cherished treasures, the loss of these narratives through memory loss related desease (e.g. Alzheimer’s or dementia) feel like losing a part of ourselves. For those grappling with these difficulties, reconnecting with familiar faces and personal objects can provide comfort, recognition, and joy.

**aTale** is designed to bridge this gap. By leveraging the power of face and objects recognition, aTale creates a personalized storytelling experience. When a familiar item is recognized, an audio story linked to it is played, enabling a unique journey of memory, emotion, and connection.

---

## Characteristics

### 1. Empathy Through Personalization
aTale tailors its storytelling to the individual. Each recognized face is associated with a unique story, fostering an emotional connection and sparking memories that might otherwise remain dormant.

### 2. Supporting Memory Recall
For individuals with Alzheimer’s or memory-related conditions, hearing stories associated with familiar faces can act as a gentle cognitive cue. This can help reinforce recognition and bring a sense of familiarity to their world.

### 3. User-Friendly and Accessible
Designed with simplicity in mind, aTale requires no technical expertise. A clean, intuitive interface ensures that anyone can use it, from tech-savvy individuals to seniors and caregivers.

### 4. Dynamic and Expandable
aTale is highly customizable. You can add new faces and objects, upload personalized stories, and adapt the experience to fit different contexts—whether it’s for a family reunion, therapeutic use, or educational purposes.


---

## Use Cases

- **Memory Care**: Supporting individuals with Alzheimer’s or dementia in reconnecting with loved ones.
- **Task aid**: Supporting individuals with Alzheimer’s or dementia in carry out simple tasks involving everyday objects.
- **Personal diary**: Attaching a story to object in order to create neverending memories.
- **Therapy Sessions**: Helping clients feel seen and heard through stories that resonate with them.

---

**aTale** is more than an app— it’s a bridge to memories, relationships, and stories that define who we are.
